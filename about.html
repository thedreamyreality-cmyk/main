ABOUT

Hi everyone, my name is Sybil and I'm a manifestation and mindset coach and content creator. My work involves helping people step into the reality they desire, instantly and effortlessly.

Growing up in a deeply religious environment, I believed in God as the creator of reality. Later, I discovered astrology and spirituality, so I decided that planets and spirits controlled reality. But what I came to understand is something that now guides all of my work: we are not separate from the universe. We are connected to everything, and our inner world shapes the reality we experience.

I believe human beings learn best through stories. Myths, movies, literature and symbols have always been humanityâ€™s way of understanding life, destiny and the self.

From the Odyssey to Alice in Wonderland, and even within the pages of the Bible, we find the same truth repeated across millennia: reality responds to belief.

Many of these stories speak of destiny, but I believe our desires are our destiny. We are not meant to follow a script thats predetermined for us, instead we are meant to write one for ourselves.

We spend our lives surrounded by stories, but we fail to realize that the most important story that we encounter is the one we tell ourselves about ourselves.

We must create our own mythology. We must become the myth-makers.
